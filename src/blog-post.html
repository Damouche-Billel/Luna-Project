Behind the Scenes of “LUNA”

May 12, 2025 · 4 min read

The world of LUNA was born from a desire to capture something raw, human, and deeply emotional. Behind every scene lies a series of choices — visual, emotional, and technical — that shaped the tone of the film.

During the early rehearsals, the cast explored the emotional weight of each moment. The chemistry between the lead actors grew naturally, creating the gentle tension that became a signature of the final film.

Creating the Atmosphere

Lighting played a crucial role in the storytelling. Our team used minimal but meaningful sources — soft lamps, natural reflections, and moonlit silhouettes — to craft the dreamy aesthetic the film is known for.

Each decision helped shape the film’s emotional voice. Even simple shots were rehearsed repeatedly to find the perfect balance between intimacy and silence.

The Final Shoot

As filming progressed, the crew became a tight-knit family. Long nights, quiet moments, unexpected improvisations — all of these contributed to the authenticity on screen.

What you see in LUNA is more than a story — it is a shared experience between artists who poured their hearts into every moment.
